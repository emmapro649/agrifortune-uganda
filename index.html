Who We Are

Rooted in Uganda.
Growing for the World.

AgriFortune Uganda was founded with a simple mission: to grow the finest bell peppers in East Africa using sustainable farming practices that respect both the land and the people who depend on it.

Located in Uganda's fertile highlands, our farms enjoy ideal soil conditions and climate — producing peppers that are consistently larger, crisper, and more vibrant than anywhere else in the region.

How It Works

From Seed to Your
Table in 4 Steps

Our rigorous process ensures every pepper that leaves AgriFortune Uganda meets the highest standards of freshness and quality.

01
🌱

Precision Planting

We select premium hybrid seeds and plant them in nutrient-rich Ugandan soil under expert agronomist guidance.

02
💧

Smart Irrigation

Drip irrigation and smart monitoring systems ensure perfect moisture levels for maximum pepper size and sweetness.

03
✂️

Hand Harvesting

Every pepper is hand-picked at peak ripeness by our trained team — ensuring zero bruising and maximum shelf life.

04
📦

Pack & Deliver

Washed, graded, and packed in our cold facility, then dispatched within 24 hours to your doorstep across Uganda.
